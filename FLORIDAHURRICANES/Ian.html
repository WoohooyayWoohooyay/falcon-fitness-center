Hurricane Ian

When it started?: Friday, September 23

Progress: Ian caused widespread damage across western Cuba and the southeast United States, especially the states of Florida and South Carolina.

Overall description:Ian caused widespread damage across western Cuba and the southeast United States, especially the states of Florida and South Carolina.

Anything unique: Hurricane Ian was one of the most powerful storms to hit the United States.

Cities impacted: Ian caused catastrophic damage with losses estimated to be more than over $50 billion. Much of the damage was from flooding brought about by a storm surge of 10–15 ft (3.0–4.6 m). The cities of Fort Myers Beach and Naples were particularly hard hit.
